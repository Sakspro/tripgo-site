Frequently asked questions

How do I cancel or change my booking?
Go to Find bookings (or My Trips in the app), open the booking you want to change, and choose Cancel or Modify. Eligibility and fees depend on the provider's policy, which is shown before you confirm.
When will I receive my refund?
Approved refunds are returned to your original payment method. Most refunds arrive within 7–14 business days, though some banks may take a little longer.
Where can I find my booking confirmation?
Confirmations are emailed to you instantly and are always available under Find bookings. Signed-in users can also view them in the app under My Trips.
How do I contact a hotel, airline or partner?
Open your booking to see the partner's contact details, or use our 24/7 live chat below and we'll reach out on your behalf.
How do I use a coupon or promo code?
Enter your code on the payment page before confirming. App-exclusive coupons are applied automatically when you're signed in.
Is my payment secure?
Yes. All payments are encrypted and processed over secure connections, and we never store your full card details.
